The Artist

As both scientist and artist, I have grown an appreciation for visual perception and the objective and subjective forms it creates for an audience. There are the emotions we feel while painting, and the emotions elicited in viewers when a piece resonates with them. The beauty of art lies in sharing our accumulated experience of the world with others and creating genuine connection.

This work represents my interest in the rules behind social behavior—"nomos"—and my exploration of these behavioral patterns not only in humans, but also in other animals and non-human primates.

Philosophy

Through Voronoi tessellations, we witness the profound dialogue between mathematical precision and natural emergence. These patterns reveal how nature solves optimization problems through simple rules, creating complex beauty from fundamental principles.

Nomos

The realm of scientific method, mathematical formalism, and human attempts to describe natural phenomena through equations and algorithms. Voronoi diagrams represent our systematic approach to understanding spatial organization.

Physis

The natural world's inherent wisdom in organizing space and resources. From cellular arrangements to territorial boundaries, nature employs Voronoi-like principles without conscious calculation.
